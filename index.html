What documents are needed for DSC?+
Individual: PAN card, Aadhaar (OTP-linked mobile), passport photo, email & mobile. Organisation: additionally GST certificate or MCA incorporation document and an authorisation letter on letterhead.
Can one DSC be used for GST, MCA & GeM?+
Yes. A single Class 3 DSC works across GST Network, MCA21, Income Tax e-filing, DGFT, GeM, EPFO, and all major e-procurement portals — as long as it is linked to the correct PAN.
What is the difference between Sign-only and Sign+Encrypt?+
Sign-only DSC covers GST, MCA, and Income Tax. Sign+Encrypt is required for portals needing data encryption — DGFT, GeM, e-Tendering. When in doubt, Sign+Encrypt offers broader compatibility.
